Welcome to InstrucAI, feel free to explore and try me! InstrucAI is an AI writing assistant that can assist you in editing your text while providing explanations of WHERE and WHY corrections were made. Additionally, it can provide examples of both casual and formal tones to help you improve the overall tone and style of your writing. Finally,it can translate your text into Chinese 成语 Chengyu language. Happy learning!
